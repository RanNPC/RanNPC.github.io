A satire newsletter for the LSU Strategic Games Club concerning stories involving the club as well as topics concerning the club. Articles are submitted by students and the finished edition is distributed to the members of the club.

Equilibrium(1.0)

I developed this first-person 3D game at the 2014 LSU GeauxHack Hackathon. Due to the nature of the Hackathons, this project will require revisions before release.

Since this was created with the free version of unity, it will be released for download without charge.

2014 Encounter Engineering Camp App

This companion app included documents and maps useful to the campers and staff of LSU's Encounter Engineering Camp. I developed this app using the intel xdk, it requiresd only minor additions to be compatible with both Iphones and Android Phones.
